Core Mechanics
The attacking team aims to plant a bomb (called the Spike) at designated sites, while the defending team tries to prevent this or defuse the Spike if planted.
remarkable maps
Designed with various strategic points, chokepoints, and bomb sites. Multiple maps, each with distinct layouts and features.
skilled Agents
